Mireia Coll is a research physiotherapist with a focus on neurological recovery. She completed her BSc in Physiotherapy in Barcelona and later an MSc in Nervous System Sciences at Rovira i Virgili University, Spain.

She has worked extensively with neurological patient populations across a range of clinical settings, before moving into clinical research with neurotechnology companies developing VR- and brain–computer interface–based approaches to stroke rehabilitation. In 2022, she joined UCL, contributing to the ArmLab led by Nick Ward. She is now part of the CMD Lab, where she is interested in studying hyperkinetic motor manifestations using multimodal datasets.
